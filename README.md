# DKC3 Practice Edition

## How To Patch

- Use the included flips program to apply the appropriate .bps patch to your unheadered (.sfc) DKC3 (US, J1.0 or J1.1) ROM.

## Main Menu Page:

- Controls:
  - DPAD: Move cursor and selections (duh)
  - L or R: Chage menu pages
  - Face buttons: Play jukebox song (with it highlighted)
  - START: Enter level (Keep it held during the fadeout to start the level with TUFST cheat).


- Warp to any level, bear houses, bird caves, or the world map.

- Warp to any bonus room or entrance in the selected level. (Can also start K.Rool 1 on either 1st or 2nd phase.)

- Set your desired Kong order represented by the Kong icons.

- Choose between p1 or p2 colors for the Kongs.

- Choose what song plays in the menu. (Press any face button after choosing the song to make it play)

**Your jukebox and color choice are saved in your cartridge.


## Settings Page

- Choose between Single Player or 2-Player Team game modes.
- Choose if your want the IL timer to always display, only when paused or during screen transitions, or turn it off.
- Choose if you want lag frames or RNG displayed in the top left.
- Choose if the input display shows on the left side of the screen, middle, right side, or turn it off.
- Choose if the game should play music or not.
- Choose which slot to use for your best times in the records page (So you can save times for more categories).
- Choose which animal (or kongs) to start the level as. The auto option will set it to what the game expects it to be.
- Choose if you want the RNG seed to be random, or use the custom seed below. (Increase/decrease the numbers with A and B respectively)

**All of these are saved in your cartridge.


NOTE: Please be mindful of turning on too many display options at once, this can, and most likely will, cause extra lag.

## Records Page

- Here you can see your best IL times for each level in the game.
- The times shown here are for your selected slot (in settings page). There are 5 different slots.
- If you wish to delete your times, press SELECT to enter delete mode, then press X to delete the selected time, or press
  SELECT again to cancel.

**All your times from all 5 slots are saved in your cartridge.

NOTE: A time will only be saved if you beat the level from the main entrance. If you got a new best time, you will see
      the "NEW RECORD!" text pop up.


## World Map

- You can warp to a few preset world map "states" (based on the  103% route). Any NPC/event/whatever flag set will be
      reset if you go back to the menu. The left side waterfall will always allow you to go into it for the 85 coins.

- Press SELECT at any point in the world map to return to the menu.

- Beating a level entered from the map will return you to the map as intended

- Banana bird caves are repeatable, going to wrinkly with all of the birds won't trigger the true ending and will just put
	you back on the map. (Saving in wrinkly will also do nothing to prevent it from nuking your save file.

## Bear Houses:

- You can warp to any bear house in the game Their flags will always be cleared and you will have the necessary items in
	your inventory for the trade, if any.

- For Bazaar you can select how many Bear coins you have (50-55), for Boomer you can select which visit to warp to for
	practicing the skips.

- On the US version, a number will be displayed below your konh to show you how many frames early or late you were
	on the skip for Boomer's 3rd visit. If you're late the number will be a different color, otherwise if early
	 it will be white.
	
- Press SELECT at any point in a bear house to return to the menu (if entered from it).


## Banana Bird Caves:

- You can warp to a banana bird cave and set how many inputs (4 through 9) you want to do in the minigame.


## In-Level Features

- START + SELECT always enabled.

- No lives deducted on death.

- Fast Retry system: Upon dying, the game will respawn you at the last exit you came out from.
  You'll respawn with the Kong order (and animal if any) you had at that time.
  You can also activate this by pressing L or R while the game is paused.

  - L will respawn you at the entrance chosen in the menu, while R will
    respawn you at the latest entrance you took in the level.

	- Your kong order, current animal, etc. are saved whenever you trigger a
	   screen transition or when touching the halfway barrel.

	- Music will not restart when Fast Retry triggers and will continue
    playing for your listening pleasure.

	- All of this also works for bonuses.


- If entered from the menu, beating a level will respawn you at the entrance chosen in it.


- Level Timer: Keeps track of your time in the level (minutes/seconds/frames).

  - Whenever you respawn/reload with the Fast Retry feature, the timer will also roll back to what it was at the time
	your stuff was preserved.

- Bleak skip counter: On either Japanese 1.0 or 1.1 versions, a number will be displayed on the top of the screen to tell
	how many frames early or late you were on the start press for Bleak skip. If you're late the number will be 
	a different color, otherwise if early it will be white.
